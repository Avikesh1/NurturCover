Benefit of the NurturCover

The policy offers more than just a funeral cover we have a monthly rewards program that will be instituted after the 3 month period and the plan premiums are paid, we will also include a discounted rate of coffins as well as tombstones as an added benefit on all plans. We have also an added program that can be added on the program once we have memberships rolling.

Why Monthly Rewards

Monthly rewards make funeral cover feel less like a cost and more like an investment—helping both the insurer retain customers and the policyholder feel valued while alive
